Programs and initiatives implemented by Nyuak Community Development Inc. in South Sudan include:

  • Providing a nutrition assistance program to combat malnutrition and non-communicable diseases among children in internal displaced persons (IDPs) camps.
  • Funding and drilling water boreholes in IDP camps to provide access to clean water.
  • Providing flood assistance services by supplying a speed boat to assist in transportation and rescue efforts in affected areas
  • Delivering batches of medicine, including medication for venomous snake bites.
  • Continue to generate funds for future activities such as provision of relief efforts

Nyuak Community Development Inc. delivers various types of tropical diseases medicine to communities in South Sudan, including medication specifically for venomous snake bites. For example, antivenom, antimalaria, Cholera drugs and other medication that might help save lives.

Nyuak Community Development Inc. is involved in peace and conflict resolution initiatives within South Sudan and its diaspora at the grass root level. Through advocacy, mediation, and community-building activities, the organization aims to facilitate dialogue, understanding, and reconciliation among various factions within the Nyuak community and society at large. Its goal is to mitigate conflicts and promote peaceful coexistence.

Nyuak Community Development Inc. provides leadership in the Nyuak community in the diaspora by serving as a central organization dedicated to resource pooling and community support. It engages in various activities such as organizing community events like fundraisng, reunion and providing support services, and facilitating communication and collaboration among Nyuak community members.
