Nyuak Community Development

Nyuak Community Development Inc. USA, is an organization founded and supported by former lost boys and lost girls of South Sudan in the US is coordinating a nutrition assistance program to help those affected by malnutrition in displaced camps. As an organization, our mission is to provide nourishment to malnourished children, elderly and pregnant women in displaced camps. NCD Inc. has delivered medical supplies to the affected populations last year. We also purchased a speed boat to facilitate transportation of sick people to where they can find medical treatment. The current project will provide nutritionally fortified flour to individuals identified to be suffering from severe malnutrition.

Nyuak Community

The Nyuak community located in Twi Central County in Jonglei State, South Sudan, consists of three clans: Awulian, Ayual, and Dachuek from the Jieng people. Bound by geography, common ancestral heritage, and socio-economic and political ties, they have coexisted peacefully for many years. They take pride in being good neighbors and uphold strong cultural values that unite them in both good and bad times. Their administrative headquarters is in Wanglei/Dung ee Chut.
