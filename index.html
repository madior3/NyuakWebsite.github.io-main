Reunification of Nyuak

Nyuak leadership in the USA pursued peace amid internal conflict, seeking counsel from community members in South Sudan. Despite initial resistance, they successfully convened a historic reunion, forming the Nyuak Community Development Corporation.



Read More



The Interim Administrative Committee of Nyuak Development Community-U.S.A

The Nyuak USA reunification process, initiated in 2016, aimed to reconcile the Nyuak community after the 2010-11 conflict between Ayual and Dachuek. Led by Awulian leaders and representatives, three years of dialogue culminated in a historic reunion on March 30, 2019, emphasizing unity, peace, and ongoing reconciliation efforts.
