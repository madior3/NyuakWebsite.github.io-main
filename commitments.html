Core Commitments

Our Core Commitments

We are defined by.

Our Mission

Our mission is to mobilize resources to advance social development through creative thinking underpinning community based planning strategy to promote cultural and social transformation through literacy in Nyuak payam in Jonglei State, (South Sudan). To mobilize resources to provide education and health services to underserved populations in Twic East county in Jonglei State, South Sudan.

Our Plan

The Nyuak Community Development organization aims to unify Nyuak community members worldwide and collaborate with like-minded communities for mutual benefits. We strive to mobilize resources from the diaspora to aid relief efforts in South Sudan while fostering unity and connectivity among Nyuak members. Furthermore, the organization seeks to enhance the quality of life in Nyuak payam by providing essential services such as clean water, healthcare, and educational support, thereby contributing to overall community well-being and development.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a safe and vibrant community.

Our Core Values

    Unity:

     We prioritize bringing our community together, both locally and globally, fostering strong bonds and shared purpose

    Collaboration:

     We value collaboration with other communities who share similar values, recognizing the importance of working together to achieve common goals and address shared challenges.

    Community Empowerment:

     We are dedicated to mobilizing resources, coordinating relief efforts, and offering vital services to enhance the well-being and quality of life for all Nyuak community members, reflecting our commitment to empowering the community.
